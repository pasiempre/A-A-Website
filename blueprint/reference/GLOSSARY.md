# Shared Glossary

Last Updated: 2026-03-21
Scope: Shared terminology across Master Spec and 90-Day Revenue Plan

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Lead | A person or company that submitted a quote request or made inbound contact; not yet a paying customer. |
| Prospect | A contacted lead that has not yet accepted a quote. |
| Customer | A lead that accepted a quote and completed at least one paid job. |
| Ticket / Job | A specific cleaning engagement with scope, schedule, and status. |
| Assignment | The link between a ticket/job and an employee or crew. |
| Quote | A formal pricing proposal sent to a lead, usually with acceptance flow. |
| Recurring Client | A customer on repeating service cadence (weekly, biweekly, or monthly). |
| SLA | Service Level Agreement: explicit response/delivery time commitment. |
| CPL | Cost Per Lead: ad spend divided by number of leads. |
| ROAS | Return On Ad Spend: attributable revenue divided by ad spend. |
| Quiet Hours | 9:00 PM to 7:00 AM local time; admin/employee automation is deferred unless explicitly exempted. |
| At-Risk Lead | Lead exceeding configured response/follow-up thresholds and requiring escalation. |
| Proof Asset | Before/after photos, case studies, testimonials, or completion evidence used for trust/conversion. |
| P0 / P1 / P2 | Delivery tiers: P0 immediate critical, P1 first 30 days, P2 90-day horizon optimization. |
